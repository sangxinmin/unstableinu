features

A 5% tax will be added to Liquidity for every buys or sells transaction to benefit HOLDERS. This feature helps HOLDERS gain rewards for holding Unstable INU long-term.

features

A 5% tax will be dedicated to the Marketing of Unstable INU. With this fee, the team will have abilities to develop Unstable INU better in the future and create more events for the community.

UnstableSwap

features

UnstableSwap will be a decentralized DeFi platform in BNB Chain, following the BEP-20 standard. UnstableSwap will be based on the architecture of Auto Market Maker (AMM), and investors will be able to Farm, Stake, and Swap tokens with the Liquidity pool.
